Friendships

On average, a softie made 4.40 new friends over coop, and 3.05 over a school term. They would lose on average 1.30 friends over coop, compared to 0.56 over a school term. Thus, the net gain in friends over coop is about 3.1 friends, while the net gain over a school term is 2.49 friends.

SE and Sexuality

A majority (62.26 %) of respondents said that they never had a sexual partner before university. That changed to only 26 % of respondents saying that they never had one during university. Someone even boasted having 9 sexual partners during their undergraduate studies.
